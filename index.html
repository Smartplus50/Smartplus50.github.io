About Us

Our company ABC ltd is a software house for developing web-based platforms for trade expos that would like a “virtual alternative” of their current set-up of a regular expo.

Instead of a “Live”& “in person” event at a physical location, Our software house develops an online system where different attendees, from around the world, can gather and experience a trade expo virtually with all the aspects of a regular expo, all in the comfort of the attendee’s web capable device of choice.

Knowledge of Issue/Client

Prior to the pandemic, face-to-face exhibitions were the norm. Many businesses will set up their trade booths to promote their business and to reach a wider audience. Recently, companies opted for virtual trade exhibitions, to prevent the spread of the covid-19.

Project Aims

Create a web-based platform for a 3D-like virtual event exhibitions.

Offer engaging features & robust data tracking measures.

Better brand visibility & increased exposure for exhibitors.

Set up trade shows booths that fit the requirements to suit exhibitor's brands image.

Simple UI/UX for attendees to understand the navigational process of the 3D-like virtual expo.

Chat functionality at trade show booths to allow seamless live chats between brands and attendees.

Data tracking to ensure digital attendee footprint.

Software Design Approach

Top down Design model

In order to get our project done, we'll be using the top-down model (stepwise refinement method)

Problems are broken down into smaller parts

We break the task we need to do in parts, and then break that part of the task into seperate parts and thus, each of parts will be easy to do.



This diagram illustrates our design process using the stepwise refinement method (top-down model).

Tasks for building the User’s section of the virtual event are divided into parts for our team to complete.

Breaking problems into parts helps identify what needs to be done and allows for easier problem-solving and collaboration.

Top-down design is suitable when designing software from scratch with unknown details.

Software Development Methodology

Considering the deadline for our project, we will be using the Agile development to develop our project.

Thus, we will be able to be more efficient as we develop through quick SCRUM sprints that we are able to iterate on.

Over the course of the project, we will be able to adjust our development objectives and monitor our progress while making any necessary changes along the way.

Product/Solution

To display our virtual product expo, we essentially make up an isometric venue to give a psudo 3d perspective.

Essentially, the attendees can move around the venue, as he can access an exhibitor's booth by heading to the nearest booth, when doing so, a new window pops up below showing exhibitor's booth item, and chat options etc.

If the attendee wants to visit another booth, they can exit by passing through them and head into a new booth.
